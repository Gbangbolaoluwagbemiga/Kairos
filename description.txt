**Problem:**  
In the rapidly evolving world of cryptocurrency, high-quality, real-time intelligence is fragmented and expensive. Traders and researchers are forced to toggle between dozens of tabs—price charts, news aggregators, on-chain explorers, and social sentiment tools—just to form a basic market thesis. Furthermore, accessing premium AI-driven insights typically requires expensive monthly subscriptions, creating a high barrier to entry for casual users.

**Solution:**  
**Kairos** is a decentralized, multi-agent AI orchestrator that acts as your personal crypto intelligence analyst for the Stellar ecosystem. Unlike generic chatbots, Kairos doesn't just "guess"; it actively coordinates a fleet of specialized sub-agents to fetch live, verifiable data from the Stellar network, Soroban contracts, and the web. Whether you need the latest SDEX volume, a breakdown of DeFi yields on Blend, or a summary of breaking news, Kairos synthesizes it all into a single, comprehensive answer.

**Unique Features & Benefits:**  
*   **Multi-Agent Orchestration**: A single query triggers parallel work from specialized agents (Stellar Scout, Price Oracle, News Scout, Chain Scout, Perp Stats, Tokenomics, and NFT Scout).
*   **Powered by x402 on Stellar**: We leverage the x402 protocol for gasless, instant USDC micro-payments between agents. This allows the main orchestrator to pay sub-agents for their work in real-time.
*   **Gasless Onboarding**: Using Stellar's Sponsored Accounts, new users can start interacting with Kairos immediately without needing XLM for base reserves.
*   **Pay-As-You-Go**: No subscriptions. Users pay a flat, transparent fee of $0.03 USDC per query directly on the Stellar Testnet.
*   **Universal Access**: Integrated with Stellar's efficient network, Kairos provides the fastest and cheapest AI-driven intelligence on the market.

**Target Audience:**  
Kairos is built for crypto traders, DeFi researchers, and enthusiasts who demand institutional-grade insights within the Stellar ecosystem without the complexity. It bridges the gap between raw data and actionable intelligence, making advanced Stellar market analysis accessible to everyone via a simple chat interface.
