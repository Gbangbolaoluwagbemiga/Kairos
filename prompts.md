## 💡 Pro Tips

- **Be specific:** "XLM price" works, but "What's the 24h change for XLM?" gives more detail.
- **Use Stellar Addresses:** Paste any 'G...' address for instant Stellar analysis.
- **Gasless Onboarding:** Ask "How do I get a sponsored account?" to learn more.
- **x402 Pulse:** Watch the UI for the 💰 Payment Pulse when agents are fetching data!
